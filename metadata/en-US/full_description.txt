This is a keyboard for people who have difficulties to correctly hit the small keyboard buttons on a small display.

Do you frequently hit the wrong buttons on your keyboard and have to delete what you have typed and try again? The intention of this keyboard is to avoid this frustrating way of typing, without relying on automatic correction (which can also get things wrong).

This keyboard uses buttons that are almost double in width, which makes it much easier to hit the correct button.
However, most keyboard buttons show two characters on them. To enter a character shown on the right, it is required to quickly tap the button twice (within 1/3 of a second).

That means, for every character you want to enter, you must briefly think and plan whether you'll have to tap once or twice.
You might be typing more slowly, however, you might be making less mistakes.
Users of devices which larger screens probably don't need this app.

To enable the keyboard:

* Open "Widekey Keyboard" from your launcher
* Enable the Widekey Keyboard (default system warning about tracking will be shown)
* Switch to Widekey Keyboard from current Input Method (differs between keyboards, usually long-press space)
* To edit Widekey Keyboard settings long-press the globe, or open system Settings, Languages &amp; Input, Widekey Keyboard.
* You can enable/disable all the input methods in Settings, Languages &amp; Input, Manage Keyboards (differs between phones)

<b>Features:</b>

* Keyboard for small screens, uses wide buttons but requires double-tapping.
* Adjustable keyboard height for more screen space
* Number row
* Swipe space to move pointer
* Delete swipe
* Custom theme colors
* Minimal permissions (only Vibrate)
* Ads-free

<b>Features it doesn’t have and probably will never have:</b>

* Emojis
* GIFs
* Spell checker
* Swipe typing
